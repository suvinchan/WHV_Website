Full job description

Costa Berries Farm in Gingin, WA 6503 is seeking casual Fruit Pickers to join our team. This role involves working outdoors in a fast-paced environment and requires attention to detail and physical endurance.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Harvest fruit from plants according to quality standards and seasonal schedules.
  • Inspect fruit for ripeness, quality, and condition.
  • Handle fruit carefully to prevent damage and ensure a high-quality yield.

Skills and Experience:

  • No previous experience required, but at least 6 months of farm work is a bonus.
  • Ability to work efficiently in outdoor conditions.
  • Physical fitness and stamina for repetitive tasks and long hours.
  • Positive attitude and the ability to work as part of a team.
  • Basic understanding of safe fruit handling and harvesting procedures.
