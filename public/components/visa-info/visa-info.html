462 Work and Holiday Visa

1. Visa Duration

  • Stay for up to 12 months
  • Work with the same employer for up to 6 months
  • Apply for up to 3 visas by meeting regional work requirements

2. Work Requirements

(2nd and 3rd Visa)
  • Complete at least 3 months of specified regional work (agriculture, tourism, construction) to qualify for a second or third visa

3. Eligibility

  • Age: 18-30 years old (35 for some countries)
  • Must hold a passport from eligible countries (e.g., Argentina, China, Spain, etc.)
  • Basic English proficiency required for some countries
  • Must show proof of AUD 5,000 in funds and have health insurance

4. Visa Benefits

  • Work and travel in Australia
  • Study for up to 4 months
  • Leave and re-enter Australia multiple times within the visa period

5. Application

  • Apply online via ImmiAccount
  • Visa fee: Around AUD 510
  • Documents: Passport, proof of funds, health insurance, possible health exams
